Frequently Asked Questions

+

How do I know the right course for me?

To clear up any ambiguity, two-week demo courses are offered, during which you will be briefed on the course and will be able to make an informed decision on which course to attend.

+

Are Online Courses available for me?

Yes, the organisation offers a variety of online classes for students who are unable to attend in person.

+

Do financial is provided by the organisation?

Yes, if a student meets the criterion for receiving financial aid, he or she will receive financial aid.

+

Conditions to get access to financial aid.?

To apply for financial help, students must come to our campus. Financial help is only given after a thorough examination of the student's legal documentation and bank statements.

+

Do I get benifitted if a refer the course to my friends?

Unfortunately, the organisation does not have such resources.

+

Is the fees refundable?

If a student believes he was enrolled in the wrong course, he can claim for a refund within a month of purchasing the course.

+

Are certificates provided by the organisation?

Yes Every student who completes the bootcamp successfully is provided a certificate.

+

Conditions for accessing my certificate?

To receive their certifications, students must follow the learning routes, be present in at least 50% of the lectures, and complete assignments on time.

+

Can I enroll into multiple courses at once?

Students are able to enroll in as many courses as they desire, but they must complete all tests and assignments by the deadlines.

+

Does resources are also provided with the course?

Yes, the tutor provides all necessary resources, as well as a few complementary additional libraries and connections for pupils to expand their knowledge.
